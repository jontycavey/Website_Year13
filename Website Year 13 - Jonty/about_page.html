What do we do here
at Jelly?

We, as a company, are souley an advertisement site for indie developers to show off their creations. This is a place where they can release behind the scenes images and spoilers for their games to create some hype.

What does it mean to
become a member?

Members have the right to freely advertise their creations using our website aslong as it doesn't break our TOS. Members will have to pay a small fee towards Jelly so we can keep this site up for them.
